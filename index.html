Here are some previews of projects I've been a part of:

Night of the Living Skurre

Night of the Living Skurre is a third-person action platformer Unity game for PC. The game relies on comedy as you play pranks on the lumberman. Complete objectives in the cabin to activate traps and watch the lumberman ragdoll.

The game is released on Steam and itch.io.


Strollmon: Walk Your Pet

Strollmon is a serious game for Android, made using Unreal Engine 5. The main idea of the game is to make your virtual pet happy by taking it on walks. Your steps act as a currency to buy outfits for your pet.

Strollmon is available at itch.io and is currently in the middle of being released to Google Play.
